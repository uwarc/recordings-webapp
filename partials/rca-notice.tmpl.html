Important Notice

This stream is provided only to members of the University of Waterloo Amateur Radio Club.

Under the Radio Communication Act of Canada, you are prohibited from rebroadcasting or discussing the items you hear on the scanner. It is also against the act to act upon the information you hear—that is you cannot go somewhere because you heard something was happening.

Members found in violation of these rules will have their access removed.

By listening to this stream, you are indicating acceptance to these rules (including the Radio Communication Act of Canada).
